Team Feedback

How does it work

From team members point of view

The new simple way to give and to receive feedback.

The new simple way to give feedback

Just grab you phone and give feedback with just a few clicks. Very simple, very quick and t makes fun. For example giving feedback to your five colleagues takes just a couple of minutes.

Do your todo lists

You will regularly get a small todo list of the reviews which your team asks you to give. Just give those reviews when it fits best to you.

Receive a lot of feedback

You will receive much more feedback than at the moment, a lot of compliments and constructive feedback. If you like, you can like or comment reviews.

Easily follow your development

Now you can easily follow how you are doing. What am I doing well? What could I maybe do better? You can also easily follow your long term development.

Summary for team members

The simple place for feedback

For you it’s the new simple way to give and to receive feedback. Do your todo-lists when it best fits to you and you receive a lot of feedback. You can easily follow how you’re doing and what you could maybe do better. Your voice will be heard. Your voice matters.
