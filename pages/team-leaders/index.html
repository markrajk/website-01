Create the right feedback routines for your team

Team leaders choose what kinds of feedback will be collected in their teams and how often. Team members get regularly a simple todo list of the reviews which they should give.

Feedback routines can be created for any types of feedback. The most common ones are the team feedback routines, reviews which are collected from all your team members. Other routines which most teams use are for example p2p feedback routines and self evaluations.

Analytic view

Understand your team and people much better

The huge amount of feedback data gives team leaders great new possibilities to understand their teams much better. Team leaders get for example answers to following questions:

How do people feel working in this team? How well is work planned and organized? How motivated are people at the moment? How productive are we? How is team work going? Who is doing well and who could maybe need some support? What are our strengths, what our weaknesses? -> How can we get forward?
